Welcome to Feel The Paint's Website

Website is currently a work in progress.



We are a woodsball paintball team from Colorado and Utah. We travel to big game and scenario events. We like to have fun and throw a lot of paint. We are the official tank crew of the FTP Tiberius Arms FIRST STRIKE Tank. If you see us at an event, come up and say hello! We love to meet and play with new people!
