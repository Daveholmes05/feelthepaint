We are a woodsball, scenario, and speedball paintball team from Colorado and Utah. We travel to several big game and scenario events across the country. We are also starting to get into the speedball scene as well.

We like to have fun and throw a lot of paint. We are the official tank crew of the FTP Tiberius Arms FIRST STRIKE Tank. If you see us at an event, come up and say hello! We love to meet and play with new people!
